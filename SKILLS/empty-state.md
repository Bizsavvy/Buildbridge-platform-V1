# Empty State Rules

This document defines the standards for all empty states within the BuildBridge platform. Empty states are critical for guiding users who may be unfamiliar with crowdfunding or have low digital literacy.

# 1. Core Principles
All data-driven pages must include an empty state to ensure the interface never feels "broken" or silent. Every empty state must satisfy the following:
* **Preserve Dignity**: Use language that encourages growth and skilled work rather than highlighting a "lack" or "need" in a pitying way.

* **Maintain Clarity**: Use short sentences and plain language at a Primary 6 reading level.

* **Drive Action**: Every empty state must include a primary action button to lead the user to the next step.

* **Visual Polish**: Use the card-based system and generous spacing consistent with the "Premium" visual direction.


# 2. Required Components
Each empty state must include:
1.  **Short Heading**: A brief, respectful message.
2.  **Contextual Explanation**: A simple explanation of why the page is empty and what happens next.
3.  **Primary Action**: A clear CTA button to initiate a workflow.


# 3. Specific Product States

### 3.1 Tradesperson: No Active Needs
* **Heading**: "Ready to grow your trade?"
* **Explanation**: "Create your first 'Need' to let backers know what equipment or materials you require to scale your business."
* **Action**: [Create a Need]

### 3.2 Backer: No Pledges Found
* **Heading**: "No pledges yet"
* **Explanation**: "Your support can change a tradesperson's day. Browse verified professionals and make your first investment."
* **Action**: [Browse Tradespeople]

### 3.3 Discovery: No Filter Results
* **Heading**: "No matches found"
* **Explanation**: "We couldn't find any needs matching these filters. Try searching for a different trade or location."
* **Action**: [Clear All Filters]

### 3.4 Impact Wall: No Stories Yet
* **Heading**: "Stories of growth are coming"
* **Explanation**: "Once a tradesperson completes their goal and uploads proof-of-use, their impact story will appear here for the community to see."
* **Action**: [View Active Needs]

### 3.5 Impact Feed (Private): No Updates
* **Heading**: "Waiting for updates"
* **Explanation**: "The tradesperson you backed will post proof-of-use here once they receive their items. We will notify you when it's ready."
* **Action**: [Back Another Tradesperson]

# 4. Implementation Standards
* **Mobile-First Layout**: Center all content for a clean, single-column mobile view.
* **Low-Bandwidth Friendly**: Use tokenized icons or CSS-based illustrations instead of heavy images to ensure the empty state loads instantly on limited data.
* **Vocabulary Check**: Never use words like "donations," "charity," or "beneficiary" in empty state copy.
