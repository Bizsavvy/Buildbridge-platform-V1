**0:00 – 0:45 | Hook & Intro**
- Millions of skilled tradespeople in Nigeria have the talent, but lack the tools.
- Traditional loans are too expensive, and personal savings take too long.
- Enter BuildBridge. 
- Let’s walk through the platform using Aisha, a talented tailor whose sewing machine just broke down. Without it, her income stops.

**0:45 – 1:45 | Onboarding, AI Need Creation & Dashboard**
- Aisha begins our seamless sign-up. 
- Right during onboarding, she creates her funding request for a new machine.
- She types a quick, rough draft of why she needs it. With one click, our built-in AI enhances her draft into a clear, compelling story.
- She finishes onboarding and lands on her Dashboard. 
- Here, she can see her request is successfully created, but currently marked as "Pending Review".

**1:45 – 2:30 | Building Trust (NIN & Vouching)**
- On BuildBridge, trust is our currency. Aisha goes to her Profile to strengthen hers.
- First, she securely inputs her NIN (National Identity Number) for official verification.
- Second, she copies her unique "Vouching Link" to share on WhatsApp. Past customers use this to publicly vouch for her skills.
- These steps directly build her Trust Badge level, proving she is a real, reliable artisan.

**2:30 – 3:15 | Platform Admin Approval**
- Now, let's briefly switch gears to the BuildBridge Admin panel.
- As an admin, our job is to keep the platform safe. 
- I review Aisha’s newly created profile, verify her submitted NIN, and review her pending sewing machine request.
- Everything checks out. I approve her NIN and approve her need. Her request is now live to the public.

**3:15 – 4:00 | Second Perspective (The Backer)**
- Let’s step into the shoes of a community backer.
- I visit the public Browse page and find Aisha’s request.
- I read her AI-enhanced story. I see her verified NIN status and her community vouches. The trust is there.
- I decide to fund her machine completely. I click pledge, and our Paystack integration handles the payment instantly and securely. 

**4:00 – 5:00 | Impact Wall & Close**
- But the journey doesn't end when the money changes hands.
- Once Aisha buys her machine, she uploads a quick video of her using it to our Impact Wall.
- I, the backer, see exactly where my money went. We don't just fund tools; we build accountability.
- By providing the right tools at the right time, we are unlocking an entire economy of skilled workers.
- BuildBridge isn’t just a funding platform. It’s an engine for economic mobility. Thank you.
